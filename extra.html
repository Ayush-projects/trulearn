FAQ

Is this for one time cost for all participant or we can use this unlimited times in a year?

It is a one time cost for one time use. If you want it multiple times you can take “Continuous 360” Product, which is a different package.

Is the per participant cost mentioned, is it for all the members who will be part of 360 Degree process or only the participant for whom the 360 Degree Feedback is being conducted?

The cost mentioned is for the participant on whom the 360 Degree Feedback is being conducted.

Will you be managing the end to end process for us or will you only provide us with the tool ?

We can do both. If required we can only provide you the tool and everything can be managed at your end, else we also have the capability to manage the end to end process for you.

Will you help us in designing the 360 Degree Questionnaire for us ?

Yes (scope of work and pricing to be mutually agreed).

Will you help us in designing the competency framework ?

Yes (scope of work and pricing to be mutually agreed).

How many admin logins will you be providing with the tool ?

One Admin login

How many times we can download the report ?

Multiple times as long as you have access to the platform.

Will these briefing and de-briefing sessions Online or In-Person ?

Both based on the option you choose.

Will you provide a template for 360 degree Questionnaire ?

Yes

How many stakeholder per participant that your tool will allow ?

Unlimited (based on the package that you chose)

Can we have a TRIAL login?

Yes you can run 360 Degree Feedback for one participant.
