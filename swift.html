Learning in the Flow of Work

  • Launch Learning Content and Learning Reinforcements as per preferred schedules
  • Reduces time required to grasp key learning by nudging to engage and remind learners to participate in learning activities
  • Let’s employees start, pause learning depending on their preferred time and convenience
  • Save and exit options built into all features for supporting learning at convenience

Socially engage cohorts

  • Peer to peer learning support in the form of instant feedback, group reflections
  • Behaviour campaigns for sharing self feedback and seeking peer feedback on behaviour change regularly
  • Leverage appreciation and healthy competition to promote peer learning process
  • Social feed with likes/ comments and Leaderboards to engage learners in social learning process

Application of learning back at work

  • “Application of learning back at work” module for sharing and reflecting on implementation of learning
  • Helps participants get real-time feedback on behaviour shifts and effectiveness of learning
  • Increases engagement of formal learning with work situations
  • QuickLearning 360 module to enable participant reflect on applications and seek peer feedback

Personalised Learning interventions

  • Makes learning process sharper and personalised to learners with the help of Heat maps, reports, dashboards of participation, progress on learning, comprehension and application
  • Options to personalise learning reinforcements, application projects at individual or group level
  • Makes progress easier to monitor and report with real time reports and dashboards

Integrate with Talent Processes

  • Multiple Potential Assessment tools to identify baseline potential level and competency gap analysis of individual candidate
  • Options to set role based, desired levels of competency proficiency
  • Supports uploading legacy performance data to create 9 box – potential performance grid
  • Continuous monitoring of IDP programs for all employees
