9 Box Talent Framework

  • Makes talent decision making faster and based on real time data by creating 9 Box – Potential and Performance grid for department, function, organisation
  • Compare different 9 box scenarios based on different combinations of criteria
  • Supports uploading legacy performance data to create 9 box – potential performance grid

Competency Gap Analysis

  • Analysis of individuals, groups’ current competency proficiency against desired levels made easy
  • Configure targeted competency levels based on roles and assess the same with multiple assessment tool options
  • Generate quick reports without manual effort with easy to export reports in excel, pdf formats for analysis

IDP Programs

  • Configure IDPs aligned to individual and organizational goals and continuously monitor progress
  • Ensure ROI for each IDP with continuous feedback options on IDP progress from manager and peer group
  • Reflection tool for employees to assess their own development helps in preventing slippages of effort on IDPs

Talent Analytics

  • Quick and easy analytics with Multiple reports and dashboards at individual and group level
  • Download Reports for assessments, competency gap analysis, 9 box framework at individual/ group level with multiple filtering options
  • Extract data from existing HR tech system and integrate with trulead data for complete talent dashboard

Integrate with Learning Processes

  • Configure learning journeys in the flow of work aligned to competency needs
  • Socially engages cohorts and leverages Informal and formal group dynamics to boost learning effectiveness
  • Increases engagement of formal learning with work situations with the help of “Application of learning back at work” module for sharing and reflecting on implementation of learning
  • Makes learning process more sharper and personalised to learners
